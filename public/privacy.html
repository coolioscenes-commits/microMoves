Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Overview

BreakBreath is a privacy-first web application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

We collect NO personal data.

Specifically:

  • No account registration required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or personal identifiers
  • No tracking cookies
  • No user profiles

Local Storage

BreakBreath stores all application data locally in your browser using localStorage and IndexedDB. This includes:

  • Your exercise preferences (environment, quietness settings)
  • Streak counters and completion history
  • Notification preferences
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any server or cloud service.

Analytics

We may use privacy-preserving analytics (such as Plausible or Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand aggregate usage patterns. These tools:

  • Do not use cookies
  • Do not collect personal information
  • Do not track you across websites
  • Are GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant

Analytics data is fully anonymized and aggregated.

Third-Party Services

BreakBreath may include links to external services:

  • Ko-fi - For voluntary donations (if you choose to support)
  • Stripe - For optional premium features (if implemented)

When you click these links, you leave BreakBreath and are subject to the privacy policies of those services. We do not receive any information about your interactions with these services.

Service Worker & PWA

BreakBreath uses a service worker to enable offline functionality. The service worker caches app resources locally on your device for faster loading and offline access. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

If you enable browser notifications, we use the browser's native Notification API. Notification permissions are controlled by your browser and can be revoked at any time in your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

BreakBreath does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no personal information, COPPA compliance is inherent.

Data Deletion

To delete all your BreakBreath data:

  1. Open the app Settings
  2. Scroll to "Reset All Data"
  3. Click "Reset" to clear all local data

Alternatively, clear your browser's local storage for breakbreath.app in your browser settings.

International Users

BreakBreath is a static web application served via a global CDN. No user data is processed or stored on servers, so data residency and cross-border transfer concerns do not apply.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@breakbreath.app
